Dusty Road

Catering

World-Class Event Catering. Dusty Road Kitchen is an established Zimbabwean company headed by Sarah. Experienced in camp and lodge kitchen consultancy, private and corporate brunches, lunches and dinners, high teas, cocktail parties, product launches, weddings, bush dinners, catering in town, country and remote areas. Benefit from our network of trusted suppliers, our international qualifications, and 25 years’ experience in catering within Africa. Competitive prices, outstanding events and superb food. Real Artisanal Food describes our approach. Wholesome yet stylish, bursting with flavour, colourful fresh and fun food is what we offer. Sarah has picked up ideas from her travels around Africa and the rest of the world, adapted them to give her cooking an Afro-fusion edge and a uniquely Zimbabwean twist, and has done this by using as many natural, indigenous and locally-grown products as possible.
